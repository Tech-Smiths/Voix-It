# 🛍️ Voix’it – Speak. List. Sell.

> 🔗 [Try the Voix’it Demo](https://hilarious-pasca-d792e1.netlify.app/) – *Experience the full flow from voice input to WhatsApp-ready product listing.*

**Voix’it** is a voice-first product listing assistant built for rural and digitally underserved vendors who struggle with traditional e-commerce tools. With Voix’it, anyone can list products on WhatsApp and create a digital catalog — using nothing but their voice.

> 🚫 No typing.  
> 🚫 No complex app.  
> ✅ Just speak — and your product goes live.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Over **400 million small-scale vendors worldwide** remain excluded from the digital economy — not because they lack products, but because they lack digital literacy.

- Many can’t read or write in English.
- Most don’t know how to type or navigate apps.
- Some don't even have consistent internet access.
- All they know is **how to speak**.

Current platforms are built for the digitally fluent. **Voix’it is built for everyone else.**

---

## 💡 What Voix’it Does

### 🔊 Voice-Driven Product Listing  
Vendors simply speak their product details — e.g.,  
*“2 kg tomatoes, ₹40 per kg.”*  
→ Voix’it extracts name, quantity, price, and category.

### 🧠 Smart Suggestions  
Auto-generated product descriptions and pricing tips using **OpenAI GPT-4**.

### 📲 WhatsApp Integration  
With one click, products are sent as a **carousel-style card** directly to WhatsApp — the platform vendors already know and trust.

### 📦 Simple 2-Page UI  
- **Add Product Page** (voice-first input)  
- **Catalog View Page** (all products listed, share/edit ready)
